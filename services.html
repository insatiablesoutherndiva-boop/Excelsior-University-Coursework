Interview preparation for transitioning service members

Preparing for an interview as a transitioning service member is a pivotal step in translating your military experience into civilian success. Begin by researching the company and role thoroughly, understand their mission, values, and how your skills align. Practice articulating your military experience in terms that resonate with civilian employers, leadership, problem solving, adaptability, and teamwork are universally valued. Use the STAR method, Situation, Task, Action, Result, to structure your responses, especially when discussing accomplishments. Dress professionally, arrive early, and bring copies of your resume. Be ready to explain why you are transitioning and what excites you about the opportunity. Be confident. Your service has equipped you with discipline, resilience, and a unique perspective that can benefit any organization.
